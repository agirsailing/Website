Who are we ?

ÄGIR Sailing Team is a student-led engineering team at KTH Royal Institute of Technology, developing a sustainable foiling Moth for the SuMoth Challenge taking place on Lake Garda. The goal is to combine engineering, sustainability, and naval design to shape the future of sailing. Led by Captain Linnea, our team of 30+ students works together every day toward one shared goal: sailing our boat this June. From bachelor to master levels, our diversity of backgrounds fuels creativity, teamwork, and shared ambition.

The SuMoth Challenge bridges the gap between classroom theory and real-world innovation. It challenges us to push the limits of performance, embrace sustainable design, and turn ideas into tangible, high-speed sailing experiences. More than a competition, it is a collaborative research platform, a space where ideas are tested, technologies validated, and the next generation of innovators gains hands-on experience in high-performance design.
